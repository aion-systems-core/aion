# How to Evaluate SealRun (Buyer Guide)

## Evaluation goals

- Verify deterministic replay quality
- Validate governance and policy enforcement
- Assess tenant isolation and access control
- Confirm enterprise operations readiness

## Recommended evaluation flow

1. Run deterministic execution and replay on representative workloads.
2. Validate drift and governance outcomes under expected/invalid scenarios.
3. Test tenant isolation with cross-tenant access attempts.
4. Review RBAC, OIDC, lifecycle controls, and legal-hold workflows.
5. Validate SIEM/OTel export and release attestation paths.

## Decision checklist

- Determinism and evidence controls meet requirements
- Governance bundles align with policy posture
- Incident/runbook/SLA model matches operations constraints
- Procurement trust artifacts are complete and reviewable
