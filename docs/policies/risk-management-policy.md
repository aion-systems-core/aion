# Risk Management Policy

## Purpose

Maintain a consistent process for identifying, rating, and treating enterprise risks.

## Policy

- Risks are recorded with likelihood, impact, and owner.
- Risk ratings: Low, Medium, High, Critical.
- Critical/High risks require treatment plans and due dates.
- Residual risk acceptance requires documented approval.
- Risk register is reviewed quarterly or after material incidents.

## Risk treatment options

- Mitigate
- Transfer
- Accept
- Avoid
