# Vendor and Third-Party Risk Policy

## Purpose

Manage security and operational risk introduced by external vendors and dependencies.

## Policy

- New critical vendors require risk review before production use.
- Security posture, data handling, and incident-notification clauses are reviewed.
- High-risk vendors are re-assessed at least annually.
- Tooling dependencies used in release/security paths (IdP, SIEM, attestation tools) are tracked in a vendor register.
- Exceptions require explicit approval and expiry.

## Minimum review fields

- Service criticality
- Data classification impact
- Authentication and access model
- Compliance posture
- Failure and contingency plan
