# Exceptions Policy

## Purpose

Define how policy and control exceptions are requested, approved, tracked, and expired.

## Policy

- Exceptions must include business justification, scope, compensating controls, and end date.
- Exceptions are approved by Security and the owning engineering manager.
- Exceptions are time-boxed and reviewed before expiry.
- Expired exceptions are treated as non-compliant unless renewed.

## Exception record template

- Exception ID
- Control/policy reference
- Risk statement
- Compensating controls
- Owner
- Approval date
- Expiry date
