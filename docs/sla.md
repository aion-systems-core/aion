# SLA Targets

## Support response and resolution targets

| Severity | Description | Initial response target | Resolution/mitigation target |
|---|---|---|---|
| SEV1 | Critical outage or security-impacting incident | 30 minutes | 4 hours mitigation |
| SEV2 | Major degradation or policy/control failure | 2 hours | 1 business day |
| SEV3 | Partial degradation, workaround available | 1 business day | 5 business days |
| SEV4 | Minor issue, documentation or non-critical defect | 2 business days | Best effort / planned release |

## Notes

- Targets apply during covered support windows.
- Compliance incidents may require additional reporting timelines.
